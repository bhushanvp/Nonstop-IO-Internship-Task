The radio station has been based on Stoke Road in Shelton since 1983 A local commercial radio station is leaving the building it has been based in for 40 years. Signal Radio's studios on Stoke Road in Stoke-on-Trent are for sale with an asking price of £600,000. The two-storey building is described as a prominent office unit with parking, which could also be rented for £53,500 a year. Owner Bauer Media has been asked what will happen to its radio shows currently broadcast from the Shelton studios. The building contains seven studios and open-plan offices  The ground floor of the building had been used for 'recording purposes', estate agent Louis Taylor said, while the second floor was an open-plan office. The 8,500 square foot site was available immediately, the agent added. Signal Radio - now known as Signal One and Greatest Hits Radio - began broadcasting from Stoke-on-Trent in September 1983.
